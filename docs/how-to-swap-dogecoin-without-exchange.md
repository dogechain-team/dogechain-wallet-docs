# How to Swap Dogecoin Without an Exchange Account

Most Dogecoin wallets only send and receive. Converting DOGE to Bitcoin, Ethereum, or stablecoins traditionally requires creating an account on a centralized exchange, completing identity verification, depositing, trading, and withdrawing. [Dogechain Wallet](https://dogechain.dev/) eliminates this process entirely with [built-in swap functionality](https://dogechain.dev/features/swap).

## How Built-in Swap Works

[Dogechain Wallet's swap feature](https://dogechain.dev/features/swap) integrates ChangeNOW's non-custodial exchange API directly into the desktop application. The process:

1. Open the Swap panel in [Dogechain Wallet](https://dogechain.dev/download)
2. Select an asset pair — DOGE → BTC, ETH, USDT, or any of 1,500+ supported assets
3. Enter the amount of DOGE to exchange
4. Provide the destination address for the target asset
5. Review the rate and confirm

Processing takes 2-5 minutes. Both fixed-rate (locked price) and floating-rate (market price at execution) options are available.

No exchange account. No identity documents. No KYC. The swap executes through ChangeNOW's non-custodial infrastructure — your funds are never held by a third party beyond the swap transaction itself.

## Why This Matters

Without built-in swap, a typical DOGE conversion requires five separate steps across two platforms. Each step introduces custody risk (your DOGE sitting on an exchange server) and friction (account creation, verification delays, withdrawal fees).

Dogechain Wallet handles everything in one application. Combined with [SPV sync](https://dogechain.dev/features/spv-sync) that gets you connected in minutes and [non-custodial security](https://dogechain.dev/features/security) that keeps keys on your device, the entire workflow — install wallet, receive DOGE, swap to BTC — takes under ten minutes.

## Supported Swap Pairs

Common DOGE swap pairs:

| From | To | Use Case |
|------|-----|----------|
| DOGE | BTC | Convert to Bitcoin |
| DOGE | ETH | Access Ethereum ecosystem |
| DOGE | USDT | Lock in dollar value |
| DOGE | LTC | Litecoin (same Scrypt algorithm family) |
| DOGE | XMR | Privacy-focused conversion |
| DOGE | BNB | BNB Chain access |
| BTC | DOGE | Acquire DOGE from Bitcoin |

Reverse swaps (BTC → DOGE, ETH → DOGE) are fully supported, allowing you to acquire DOGE from other cryptocurrencies directly into your wallet.

## Swap Fees and Pricing

Fees are included in the displayed exchange rate. What you see before confirming is what you receive — no hidden charges, no post-transaction deductions. Network fees for the Dogecoin transaction (typically under $0.001 USD) apply separately and are displayed before confirmation.

ChangeNOW aggregates liquidity from 10+ centralized and decentralized exchanges to provide competitive rates across all supported pairs.

## When to Swap vs When to Use an Exchange

**Built-in swap is better for:**
- Moderate amounts without KYC delays
- Users who don't want exchange accounts
- Quick conversions (2-5 minutes)
- Privacy-conscious users

**Traditional exchanges are better for:**
- Large volume trading (order book depth)
- Fiat withdrawal (swap is crypto-to-crypto only)
- Advanced features (limit orders, margin)
